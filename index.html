About Me

I am a researcher with a background in biochemistry and a focus on studying enzymes in extreme environments. My research aims to understand microbial responses and their applications in astrobiology. I have presented my work at various conferences, including the Goldschmidt Conference and the Astrobiology Graduate Conference.
